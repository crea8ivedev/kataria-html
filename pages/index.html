1991

The Beginning Of A Vision

Founded in 1989, Kataria Enterprise today operates across 7 states and 280+ districts, serving over 10 lakh retailers and reaching over 67 crore people across India. We aim to deliver value-driven, cost-effective, and innovative logistics and marketing solutions that exceed customer expectations.
